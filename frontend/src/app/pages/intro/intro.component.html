What is Support Board?

Support Board is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline the process of handling and managing requests within an organization. It is particularly beneficial in IT companies where it serves as a central system for tracking and resolving issues, managing service requests, and ensuring smooth operations.

Where is it Used?

IT Support

For handling technical issues, software installation requests, and system modifications.

HR Departments

For managing onboarding processes, employee requests, and internal communications.

Facilities Management

For managing maintenance requests and facility-related issues.

How to Use

Logging In

Users log into the system using their credentials.

Raising a Ticket

Users raise the ticket by detailing their request.

Assigning the Ticket

The ticket is received by the relevant department. The department head reviews the ticket and assigns it to an appropriate employee.

Processing the Ticket

The assigned employee starts working on the request, updates the ticket status, and communicates progress.

Closing the Ticket

Once the request is fulfilled, the employee closes the ticket, marking it as resolved.
